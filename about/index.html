About

May 26, 2016
1 min read

Hi, I’m Oli and I work as a software engineer in San Francisco.

I spend a lot of time writing ruby code for Flexport to automate and scale tasks such as boat tracking, boat timetables and cost prediction. Before that I spent several years down the road at Square working on logistics problems. My primary focus wherever I go is getting stuff done by writing software efficiently and applying it wisely to areas that haven’t fully benefited from it so far.

I’m a believer in technology, but also digital minimalism, and am passionate about finding that balance between the two.

I owe my footing in this world to loving parents, supportive teachers, a Randy Pausch lecture and the earnestness of youth which took me to London to study Computing at Imperial College, then France to intern (and have the summer of a lifetime) and then a pilgrimage to San Francisco which I’ve happily called home for the past six years.
